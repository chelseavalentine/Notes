### Big numbers

* 10,000 is suffixed by 만
* 1,000 is suffixed by 천
* 100 is suffixed by 백

Therefore, 234,567 is = 23 x 10,000 + 4 x 1,000 + 5 x 100 + 6 x 10 + 7 x 1 = 이십삼만, 사천, 오백, 육십, 칠
